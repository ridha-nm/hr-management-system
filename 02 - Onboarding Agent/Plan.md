## 🎯 **Purpose**

Automated new hire onboarding with 16-step workflow covering HRMS setup, IT equipment, meetings, and team integration.

---

## 📋 **Features**

### **16-Step Onboarding Workflow**

1. ✅ Create employee record in HRMS
2. ✅ Generate employee ID
3. ✅ Send welcome email with first-day instructions
4. ✅ Notify manager and team
5. ✅ Create IT equipment ticket (laptop, monitor, accessories)
6. ✅ Schedule orientation meetings
7. ✅ Assign onboarding buddy
8. ✅ Share company policies
9. ✅ Collect required documents
10. ✅ Set up payroll account
11. ✅ Create access cards
12. ✅ Add to communication channels (Slack, Email groups)
13. ✅ Schedule 1:1 with HR
14. ✅ Department introduction meetings
15. ✅ Training plan creation
16. ✅ 30-60-90 day check-in reminders

### **Commands**
- `onboard [name]` - Start onboarding workflow
- `onboarding status [employee]` - Check onboarding progress
- `onboarding checklist` - View onboarding tasks
- `assign buddy [employee]` - Assign onboarding buddy
- `schedule orientation` - Schedule orientation sessions

---

## 🔗 **Integrations**

- **HR Generalist Agent:** Employee record creation
- **IT Support Agent:** Equipment ticket creation
- **Interview Agent:** Meeting scheduling
- **EP Agent:** Foreign employee onboarding
