## 📁 Document Checklist

### Phase 1: Company Documents ⏳

**Contact:** Syaheedah (COO) - syaheedah@pantas.com

- [ ] **SSM Copy** (latest; printing date within 6 months)
  - 📄 Template: `../Legal Company Documentation/`
  - 📧 Email Template: See "Email Templates" section below
  
- [ ] **Company License (Lesen)**
  - 📄 Location: `../Legal Company Documentation/Lesen (1).pdf`
  
- [ ] **Directors' IC/Passport Copies** (all listed in SSM)
  - 📧 Request from: syaheedah@pantas.com
  
- [ ] **Staff Breakdown Confirmation**
  - Top Management: 3
  - Middle Management: 6
  - Entry Level: 23
  - Starting Salary: RM 3,800

---

### Phase 2: Candidate Documents ⏳

- [ ] **Passport Copy** (all pages, valid 15+ months)
  - 📋 Requirement: Minimum 15 months validity
  
- [ ] **CV/Resume** (updated)
  - 📋 Must include: Education, Work Experience, Skills
  
- [ ] **Educational Certificates** (with CTC stamps)
  - 📋 Requirement: Certified True Copy (CTC) stamp required
  
- [ ] **Passport Photo** (light blue background)
  - 📋 Requirement: Light blue background, neutral expression
  
- [ ] **IRB Tax Clearance** (if worked in Malaysia before)
  - 📋 Only required if candidate has previous Malaysian work experience

---

### Phase 3: MyFutureJobs (PERKESO) ⏳

**Portal:** https://www.perkeso.gov.my/pengiklanan-myfuturejobs-bagi-penggajian-pegawai-dagang.html

- [ ] **Create MyFutureJobs Account**
  - 🔗 Link: PERKESO portal
  
- [ ] **Post Job Advertisement** (minimum 14 days)
  - 📄 Template: `../docs/MyFutureJobs_GUIDE.md`
  - 📋 Position: Sustainability Data Analyst
  
- [ ] **Conduct Interviews**
  - 📄 Template: `../docs/INTERVIEW_TEMPLATE.md`
  - 📋 Minimum 14 days interview period
  
- [ ] **Submit Interview Assessment**
  - 📄 Form: `../docs/INTERVIEW_TEMPLATE.md`
  
- [ ] **Receive Approval Letter** (5-7 working days)
  - 📁 Save to: `MyFutureJobs/Approval Letter/`

---

### Phase 4: JTKSM Application ⏳

- [ ] **Generate JTKSM Report**
  - 📄 Template: `../templates/jtksm_template.docx`
  - 📋 Auto-fill with candidate data
  
- [ ] **Submit JTKSM Application**
  - 📋 Include: JTKSM Report, Company Documents

---

### Phase 5: EP Application ⏳

- [ ] **Generate GP Checklist**
  - 📄 Template: `../templates/gp_checklist_template.docx`
  
- [ ] **Submit EP Application to MDEC/ESD**
  - 🔗 Portal: https://esd.imi.gov.my/
  - 📋 Include: All approved forms, candidate documents

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: How long does the entire process take?**  
A: Approximately 2-3 weeks (MyFutureJobs: 2 weeks, EP processing: 4-6 weeks)

**Q: What if passport expires in less than 15 months?**  
A: Candidate must renew passport before applying

**Q: Is Tax Clearance always required?**  
A: No, only if candidate previously worked in Malaysia

**Q: What if certificate doesn't have CTC stamp?**  
A: Get it certified at institution, notary public, or Commissioner of Oaths
