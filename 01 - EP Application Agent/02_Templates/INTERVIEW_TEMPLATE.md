# Interview Assessment Template

**Purpose:** Template for documenting candidate interviews during MyFutureJobs application

**Position:** Sustainability Data Analyst

**Last Updated:** March 25, 2026

---

## 📋 How to Use This Template

### Section 1: Job Details (Stays the Same)
- Fill in once per job posting
- Reuse for all candidates
- Only change if job details change

### Section 2: Candidate Assessment (Edit Per Candidate)
- Fill in for each candidate interviewed
- Add pages if more than 10 candidates
- Document reasons for selection/rejection

### After Interviews
- Submit through MyFutureJobs platform
- Bot can help fill the form once interviews are complete
- Save to: `MyFutureJobs/Interview Assessment/`
