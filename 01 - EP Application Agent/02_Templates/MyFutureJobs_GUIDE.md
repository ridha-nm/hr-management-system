## 🚀 MyFutureJobs Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Before Posting

#### Documents Needed
- [ ] Company SSM registration
- [ ] Job description for position
- [ ] Company details (staff breakdown, salary)
- [ ] SOCSO registration number

#### Information from Legal Folder
- **Reference:** `Legal Company Documentation/SOCSO (Perkeso) approval. Steps (2025).pdf`
- **Staff Breakdown:** See above (3 Top, 6 Middle, 23 Entry)
- **Starting Salary:** RM 3,800

---

### Step 2: Create MyFutureJobs Account

1. **Visit:** https://www.perkeso.gov.my/
2. **Navigate:** MyFutureJobs portal
3. **Register:** Company account
4. **Verify:** SOCSO registration

---

### Step 3: Create Job Posting

#### Job Details Template

**Position:** Sustainability Data Analyst

**Job Description:**
```
Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain data pipelines for sustainability metrics
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and insights
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Work with cross-functional teams to implement data-driven solutions

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in data analysis
- Proficiency in SQL, Python, and data visualization tools
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication skills in Bahasa Malaysia and English

Location: [City, Malaysia]
Salary Range: RM [X,XXX] - RM [X,XXX]
Employment Type: Full-time
```

**Company Information:**
- **Company Name:** Pantas Climate Solutions
- **SSM Registration:** [From SSM zip file]
- **Local Staff:** 32 (3 Top + 6 Middle + 23 Entry)
- **Starting Salary:** RM 3,800

---

### Step 4: Submit Job Posting

1. **Log in** to MyFutureJobs portal
2. **Create New Posting**
3. **Fill in:**
   - Job title
   - Job description
   - Requirements
   - Salary range
   - Location
4. **Submit** for approval

---

### Step 5: Interview Period (14 days minimum)

#### During Interview Period
- [ ] Schedule candidate interviews
- [ ] Use Interview Template (see below)
- [ ] Document interview outcomes
- [ ] Keep records of all candidates

#### Interview Documentation
**Use:** Interview Template (next section)
- First section: Job details (stays same)
- Second section: Candidate details (edit per candidate)
- Add pages if more than 10 candidates

---

### Step 6: Complete Interview Assessment

#### After Interviews
1. **Fill up** Interview Assessment Form
2. **Document** reasons for selection/rejection
3. **Submit** through MyFutureJobs platform
4. **Wait** for approval letter

#### Processing Time
- **Review:** 5-7 working days
- **Approval:** Expected within 2 weeks

---

### Step 7: Receive Approval Letter

#### Once Approved
- [ ] Download approval letter
- [ ] Save to: `MyFutureJobs/Approval Letter/`
- [ ] Use for: JTKSM application
- [ ] Use for: EP application
