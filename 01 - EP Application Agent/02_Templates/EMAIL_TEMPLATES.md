# Email Templates for EP Applications

**Purpose:** Ready-to-use email templates for requesting documents and communicating with agencies

**Last Updated:** March 25, 2026

---

## 📧 Template 1: Request Documents from COO

**When to use:** When you receive EP application requirements and need company documents

**To:** COO / Company Secretary  
**Subject:** Documents Required for EP Application - [CANDIDATE_NAME] ([POSITION])

```
Dear [COO Name],

I hope this email finds you well.

We are proceeding with the Employment Pass (EP) application for our candidate:

**Candidate Name:** [CANDIDATE_NAME]
**Position:** [POSITION]
**Application Type:** New EP + JTKSM + PERKESO

To complete the application, we require the following company documents:

=== COMPANY DOCUMENTS ===

1. Latest SSM Copy
   - Printing date must be within 6 months
   - Required for: JTKSM application

2. Directors' IC or Passport Details
   - For all directors listed in SSM
   - Required for: Company verification
   - **Action:** Please send directly to agency

3. Signed Offer Letter / Employment Contract
   - Management Level (2 pax) + General Worker (2 pax)
   - Required for: JTKSM local staff verification
   - **Action:** Please send directly to agency

=== LOCAL STAFF BREAKDOWN ===

Please provide the following breakdown:

- Top Management: ___ pax
- Middle Management: ___ pax
- Entry Level: ___ pax
- Starting Basic Salary for Local Malaysia Staff: RM ___

=== TIMELINE ===

We aim to submit the application by [DATE]. Could you please provide these 
documents by [DATE - 3 days]?

=== NEXT STEPS ===

Once we receive these documents:
1. Generate JTKSM Laporan Pengambilan
2. Submit MyFutureJobs application
3. Complete EP application submission

Please let me know if you have any questions or need clarification on any 
of the required documents.

Thank you for your support.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Position]
[Contact Information]
```

## 📧 Template 6: Request Candidate Documents

**When to use:** Initial request to candidate for EP application documents

**To:** Candidate  
**Subject:** EP Application - Required Documents

```
Dear [CANDIDATE_NAME],

Congratulations on your selection for the [POSITION] position!

We are now proceeding with your Employment Pass (EP) application. To 
complete the application, please provide the following documents:

=== REQUIRED DOCUMENTS ===

1. Passport Copy (all pages)
   - All pages including blank pages
   - Clear color scan

2. Passport-Sized Photo
   - Light blue background
   - Neutral expression
   - Recent photo (within 3 months)

3. CV/Resume
   - Updated with latest experience
   - Include all educational qualifications

4. Educational Certificates
   - All degree/diploma certificates
   - Must have "Certified True Copy" (CTC) stamp
   - Clear color scans

5. Academic Transcripts
   - Official transcripts from all institutions
   - Clear color scans

=== SUBMISSION ===

Please provide all documents by [DATE] in PDF format.

=== IMPORTANT NOTES ===

- All documents must be clear color scans
- Certificates must have CTC stamps
- Passport must be valid for at least 15 months
- Photo must have light blue background

If you have any questions or need clarification, please contact me.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Position]
[Contact Information]
```
