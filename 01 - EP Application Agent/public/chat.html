Employment Pass Automation

Complete guide for Employment Pass applications in Malaysia. Download documents, upload interview data, and get instant answers.

Phase 1
Company Documents
⏱️ 1-3 days

Download SSM, DBKL, and other company documents. Send to immigration agency.

Phase 2
Candidate Documents
⏱️ 1-2 weeks

Collect passport, CV, certificates, and photo from candidate. Verify requirements.

Phase 3
MyFutureJobs PERKESO
⏱️ 2-3 weeks

Post job, conduct interviews (14 days min), upload candidate data, generate form.

Phase 4
JTKSM Application
⏱️ 1 week

Fill MyFutureJobs Expat Form, get COO signature, submit to JTKSM.

Phase 5
EP Application ESD
⏱️ 4-6 weeks

Submit GP Checklist and all documents to ESD portal. Wait for approval.
